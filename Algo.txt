1. Decide what business to order from.
2. Visit that business's website.
3. Select the style of pizza you desire.
  a. Select a Size (Small, Medium, Large, Extra Large)
  b. Select a Crust Style (Deep Dish, Regular, Thin Crust)
  c. Select Sauce (Red Sauce, Alfredo)
  d. Select Meats (Bacon, Beef, Pepperoni, Sausage)
  e. Select Additional Toppings (Bell Peppers, Olives, Onions)
4. Enter delivery address information.
5. Enter payment information.
6. Submit order.
7. Wait 20 minutes.
  a. If pizza has arrived, eat delicious pizza.
  b. If pizza hasn't arrived, make an angry phone call.
